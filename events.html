Fifth Welfare Project

Date - 05.05.2018

With the guidance of our Guru the 5th Welfare project was celebrated on 05.May.2018 at Galleface Green, where 110 Wheel Chairs were distributed to differently able Army, Police and Civilians.

Fourth Welfare Project

Date - 12.01.2018

With the guidance of our Guru the 4th Wlfare project was held on 12-01-2018 at our Trust premises where Wheel chairs and Walking aids were distributed to 100 person.

Third Welfare Project

Dry Ration Distribution - 2016

Date - 23rd, 24th and 25th of July

To commemorate the Birthday of His Holiness Sulthanul Arifeen, Sheihul Mufliheen M.S.M.Abdullah (Rah) by distributing dry rations (Feast) by the disciples according to the schedule given by His Holiness which occurs every year on 23rd,24th and 25th 0f July. This occasion starts with hoisting the flag on 23rd of July after distributing the dry rations.

Second Welfare Project

Date - 13-01-2017

2nd Welfare Project on 13th of January 2017 at the Trust Premises for selected 100 elderly from the Tamil and Muslim communities in Aarayampathy and Kaththankudy area by distributing clothing and providing a sumptuous meal. Following that Islamic devotional songs were sung by Eahaththuvakkural James Kennedy from Trichy, India.
